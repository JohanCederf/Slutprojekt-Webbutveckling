Transistor

You play as Red, a singer in the city of Cloudbank,who uses the Transistor to fight the Process, and to bring the Camerata to justice. It begins after Red and her companion narrowly escaped an attack by the Camerata. Red retrieves the Transistor, who narrates the game, from the body of a nearby man.
